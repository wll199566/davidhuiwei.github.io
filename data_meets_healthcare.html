About

    Data Meets Healthcare seminar series happens in on biweekly Monday at 2:00-3:00pm. This seminar right now is organized entirely online via Zoom meeting. The format is to invite researchers to give talks about their works combining data and healthcare. Our ultimate goal is to learn and communicate with each other to make digital healthcare and medicine more accurate and robust.
